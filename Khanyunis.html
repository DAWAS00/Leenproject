Targeting Him:

Wael survived an Israeli bombardment that targeted him and his colleague,
photographer Samer Abu Daqqa, while they were trying to transmit pictures of
the destruction caused by an Israeli bombardment on a school in Khan Yunis.
This resulted in Wael sustaining various injuries and the martyrdom of his colleague,
photographer Samer.
