Sign and Symptoms of a Painic Attacks

  1. pounding or racing heartbeat.

  2. feeling faint, dizzy or light-headed.

  3. feeling very hot or very cold.

  4. sweating, trembling or shaking.

Here are some common reasons (causes)
for a panic Attacks

  1. Stress – High levels of stress from work, relationships, or life changes.

  2. Anxiety disorders – Generalized anxiety, social anxiety, or other related conditions.

  3. Trauma – Past traumatic experiences or PTSD.

  4. Phobias – Being exposed to a specific fear (like flying, heights, etc.).

  5. Substance use – Caffeine, drugs, or alcohol can trigger panic attacks.
